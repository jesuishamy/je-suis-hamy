My name is HAMY

Allow me tell you a little bit about my story…

After successfully completing my ABIBAC (a Franco-German double diploma combining the baccalauréat and the German Abitur) at the Lycée Honoré d'Urfé in Saint-Etienne, I opted for a completely different language to further my studies: Japanese...

In September 2017, I found myself at the University of Strasbourg, starting my LLCE Japanese bachelor (Foreign language, litterature and culture studies). At the end of this degree, I turned to another type of language: computer language...

In September 2021, I begin my adventure with CAWEB, a master's degree in multilingual web communication, which combines web development, UI/UX, localization, digital marketing, project management and desktop publishing. In the end, it's the latter that has captured my heart, as I'm now training to become a graphic designer at EDAA, a distance learning school of applied arts!

Now I know how to do some stuff:

Web development
HTML, CSS, JavaScript

DTP
Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator

Languages
French, English, German, Malagasy, Japanese

My goal is to use all these tools for the benefit of others, for causes that are close to my heart. My dream is to become rich from what I give!
